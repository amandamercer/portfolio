DEVANAR 2000

Baitcaster Reel

7-bearing system includes Powerlock instant anti-reverse. Aluminum frame. Double-anodized- aluminum spool with line pin. Adjustable Dual Braking System. Carbon washer drag system. Cast control knob. Power Crank handle with Soft Touch PVC knobs.

Pesci® DEVANAR 2000 Baitcaster Reels are great for fishing umbrella-style rigs, swimbaits, or for any other situation that requires extra line capacity, or wherever a strong, low-profile reel is preferred.
